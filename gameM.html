How To Play

Objective

Each Player has two balls which will refresh after both balls have been released by the player and exited the field. The first person to hit the other WINS!!!! If one catches a ball(the ball has to land in between the two platforms) the player gains an extra ball. Good Luck and Have Fun!

Online Controls:

Right Arrow and Left Arrow
Up Arrow to Shoot
